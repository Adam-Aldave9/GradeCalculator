Grade Calculation

A weighted grade is calculated by the sum of the products of grades
and their corresponding weights divided by the sum of the weights.

Weighted grade = (g1×w1+g2×w2+g3×w3+...) / (w1+w2+w3+...)
Both the weights and grades used should be in percentage form.

Example

Calculating your Final Grade in your Math course:
  • Assignments are worth 30%, and your overall grade for them is 70%
  • Midterm is worth 30%, and your overall grade for it is 80%
  • Exam is worth 40%, and your overall grade for it is 60%
Weighted grade = (g1×w1+g2×w2+g3×w3) / (w1+2+w3)
= [(70)×(30)+(80)×(30)+(60)×(40)] / (30+30+40)
= 6900 / 100
= 69

Example

Calculating your Overall Grade on a Test:
  • Section 1 is worth 25%, and your overall grade for it is 81%
  • Section 2 is worth 20%, and your overall grade for it is 90%
  • Section 3 is worth 40%, and your overall grade for it is 56%
  • Section 4 is worth 15%, and your overall grade for it is 63%
Weighted grade = (g1×w1+g2×w2+g3×w3+g4×w4) / (w1+2+w3+w4)
= [(81)×(25)+(90)×(20)+(56)×(40)+(63)×(15)] / (25 + 20 + 40 + 15)
= 7010 / 100
= 70.1
